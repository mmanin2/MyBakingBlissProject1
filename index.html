Welcome to My Baking Bliss!

About the Chef!

I’m Jess, a twenty-something self-taught baker, who finds her bliss in the kitchen. I created this blog to share my passion and prove that anyone can become a great baker with a little confidence, a lot of practice, and a whole lot of butter and sprinkles. I began baking at a young age, helping my mom in the kitchen. Before I knew it, I was in charge of all of our holiday baking, and my passion for baking exploded. I love sharing my desserts with family and friends, and wanted to share my love and knowledge with everyone!

Here on My Baking Bliss, you’ll find my favorite recipes, from family staples I made as a kid, to new and innovative twists on classic desserts. Along the way, I’ll share my tips and tricks for perfect cookies, fluffy cupcakes, and even staple equipment and ingredients to give you all the tools needed to become successful in the kitchen. I’m here to show that baking should be seen as fun and enjoyable, not intimidating and overwhelming.

Other forms of bliss include chai lattes, concerts, binge-watching The Office, cookie butter, and spending time with my husband, Mitchell, and our fluff ball cat, Molly.
